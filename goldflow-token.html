GoldFlow Token

Responsible Digital Gold.

GoldFlow implements blockchain technology and decentralized infrastructure to digitize vaulted gold; enabling users to store and transact from their mobile digital wallet. GoldFlow Token is a digital asset backed by physical gold – each GFT represents 1 gram of 99.99% Gold, reserved in Sequoyah vaults.
