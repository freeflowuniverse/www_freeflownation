Frequency Village

Using technology, innovation and community for a harmonious planet


Frequency Village is a global community and innovation lab designed to develop and support earth-positive solutions to realign humanity and technology with nature while being good stewards of the planet and one another. With an intentional and collaborative network of safe and transformative spaces that serve as hubs of planetary innovation and positive impact, we are creating a blueprint for a new ecosystem of human and planetary flourishing.

At the core of Frequency Village is the Planetary Innovation Lab, a venture studio and R&D hub that provides strategic capital and resources to support founders working on innovative projects and technology with real-world impact. The Lab validates and nurtures these solutions while providing the space, lifestyle and culture for inner and outer transformation.
