ThreeFold

Empowering sustainability, freedom, & equality

ThreeFold envisions a green and responsible peer-to-peer Internet that is available everywhere and owned by everyone – without borders nor geographical discrimination, empowers people to be digitally independent, and provides equal chances to learn, partake and succeed.

LEARN MORE

What is the problem?

Today's Internet is built on centralized and power-hungry data centers, leaves users without control of their data, and is inaccessible to about 50% of the world. We deserve a better Internet.

How are we helping to resolve?

We are building an Internet that empowers sustainability, freedom, and equality.

The ThreeFold Network is the first true peer-to-peer Internet. It uses pioneering technologies that remove the need for data centers, consumes 90% less energy, and uses 90% less international fibre network capacity. It enables every individual to have full ownership of their data. For a cost of $1 per month, users can have full control of where to store their data and with whom to share. And by deploying a distributed grid of nodes, remote regions can now be reached in a natural progression. Being decentralized by nature and leveraging self-healing technologies, the ThreeFold Grid cannot be restricted or sectioned off. This leads to a substantial increase in efficiency, access, and affordability.
