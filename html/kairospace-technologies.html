Solutions

1

Safely stored hydrogen on demand

Decentralized storage of energy has become an important topic. With safely stored hydrogen on demand, we can turn locally produced electricity from renewables into hydrogen and store it safely and for a long time without any loss. On demand, the hydrogen can quickly be released and converted back into electricity. 

2

High-eﬃciency, scalable wastewater and desalination units  

High-efficiency water desalination is a process for maximum recovery of water from a waste water source that would otherwise be discharged. It is a water treatment process in which all wastewater is purified and recycled; therefore, leaving zero discharge at the end of the treatment cycle. Additionally, we produce cost effective and reliable solar-driven desalination system for regions that suffer water shortages.

Impact

1

More efficient clean energy infrastructure

We have expertise and access to a suite of sustainable water and energy-related technologies with which we are able to lead projects of powerful planetary impact, such as water remediation, mining, sanitation and desalination as well as building clean energy infrastructures that involve water processes.

2

Reduces reliance on harmful chemical processes for water treatment

We focus on technology that uses electromagnetic frequency induction to send targeted frequencies generated through electro-magnetic pulses to fluids and gases, such as water and hydrogen, to modify their physiochemical properties and structure, replacing traditionally harmful chemical methods of treating fluids with an efficient and sustainable physical process. 

3

Cleaner water and waterways

Bioremediation is an eco-friendly and efficient method of reclaiming environments contaminated with heavy metals by making use of the inherent biological mechanisms of microorganisms and plants to eradicate hazardous contaminants. Water remediation via micro and nano bubble sonication. Nanobubbles, with their unique physical properties, show a very strong potential to optimize water and energy related industrial processes.
