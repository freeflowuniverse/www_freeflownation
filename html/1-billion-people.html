How can we get to One Billion?

According to The World Bank, nearly half of the world lives on less than $5.50 per day. A similar number of people are not classified as Internet users (with only 16% of schools having Internet access in the least developed countries) and 1.7 billion adults, or more, remain unbanked.

Here's how we all can help to change that:

Existing projects

Planet First Alliance projects are already making an impact across the globe – education, Internet, and finance sector. Innovation Lab Schools has nearly 1,000 teachers involved around the world, and connects students in Africa with schools across 90 countries via video conferencing. The ThreeFold Network has 40 million gigabytes of responsible internet capacity online globally today, and growing and Kleos is working on a better 5G solution. Seeds has created a financial system with planet regeneration as its backbone. And the Talamanca Land Bank is empowering local farmers in Costa Rica, and developing a token that will be backed by one square meter of the rainforest. It is all coming together.
